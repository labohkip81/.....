Working at HassConsult

At Hassconsult Real Estate, we are  constantly improving and expanding the skills available in our team. We also provide an excellent growth environment for our staff members.

You can view and apply online for current openings HassConsult Real Estate. We are fortunate to receive a considerable number of applications from highly qualified individuals. We contact only those candidates who best match the position requirements to begin the interview process.

You are welcome to apply for more than one position. HassConsult Real Estate is an equal opportunity employer. Please note that we do not charge fees from applicants during the recruitment process.

Sales Manager

Job Summary

We are looking for Sales Manager to direct the team's focus, set goals, look at data, motivate sales team, and determine ways to streamline and improve the sales process. Responsibilities

  • 1.Develops sales strategy for the market that ensures attainment of company sales goals and profitability.
  • 2. Responsible for the performance and development of the team.
  • 3. Prepares action plans for effective search of sales leads and prospects.
  • 4. Developing initiatives to develop new leads and increase sales for the company
  • 5. Initiates and coordinates development of action plans to penetrate new markets.
  • 6. Provides timely feedback to senior management regarding performance.
  • 7. Provides timely & accurate reviews from consumers on all development properties
  • 8. Maintains accurate records of all pricings, sales, and activity reports submitted by team.
  • 9. Creates and conducts proposal presentations.
  • 10. Oversee proper classification of all sales documents and maintains detailed reports on each sales project.
Job Requirements

Required education: Bachelor's degree
Required relevant work experience: 3 years
Required skills: Market research, intelligence; Business development; Pricing, price management; Sales strategy; Sales performance tracking and reporting
Required languages: English (Spoken: fluent | Written: fluent)
