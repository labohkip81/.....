Westlands - Off Muthangari Drive

Westlands
5 bed Villa
Kshs 55m
Facebook Twitter Google +
Expand All
 

Boasting a prime location, immediately off Muthangari drive is this 5 bedroom townhouse with spacious living in a compound of 6. Accommodation is spread in 2 levels with the ground floor compromising of a lounge cum dining area with a lovely view of the garden. The dining room gives way to a separate kitchen and spacious pantry leading to the laundry area and sq. on this same level we have the guest bedroom with access to the cloak room from the room.

The first level comprises of an open family room with 4 rooms, 2 sharing a bathroom while one is ensuite and the other being the master bedroom. The master bedroom has a walk-in closet, bathtub and a balcony with the view of the garden.

Finishes include granite worktops in the kitchen, private garden, sq and 24 hour security.
